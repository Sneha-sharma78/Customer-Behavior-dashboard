# Customer-Behavior-dashboard
📊 Customer Behavior Analytics Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze customer purchasing patterns, subscription behavior, and sales performance across different categories, demographics, and shipping preferences.
The Customer Behavior Dashboard is a data-driven visualization tool that helps businesses understand customer trends, purchasing habits, and revenue distribution. It enables better decision-making by analyzing customer segments, product categories, and sales performance.
Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• 📊 Power BI Desktop – Main platform used for creating interactive dashboards
• 📂 Power Query – Used for data cleaning and transformation
• 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used for KPIs like average purchase, customer count, and rating calculations
• 🗂️ Data Modeling – Relationships between customer data, transactions, and product categories
• 📁 File Format – .pbix for development and .png for preview
Data Source

Source: Simulated / App-based customer transaction data

The dataset includes:

Customer details (gender, subscription status)
Purchase transactions
Product categories (Clothing, Accessories, Footwear, Outerwear)
Shipping preferences
Ratings and reviews

The data is structured into multiple tables such as:

Customers
Orders / Transactions
Categories
Reviews
Business Problem

Businesses often struggle to understand:

1.Which customer segments generate the most revenue
2.How subscription status impacts sales
3.Which product categories perform best
4.What demographic groups contribute most to growth

Raw data makes it difficult to quickly identify trends and take action.
Goal of the Dashboard

To provide an interactive tool that:

1.Tracks customer behavior and purchase patterns
2.Helps analyze revenue across categories
3.Identifies high-value customer segments
4.Supports data-driven business decisions
Business Impact & Insights

📈 Customer Targeting:
Businesses can focus on young adults, who generate the most revenue.

💰 Revenue Optimization:
Clothing category is the top performer → increase inventory & promotions.

📊 Subscription Growth Opportunity:
With only 27% subscribed users, there is strong potential to improve loyalty programs.

🚚 Operational Insights:
Shipping preferences help optimize delivery strategies.

🎯 Marketing Strategy:
Personalized campaigns can be created based on gender, age group, and purchase behavior.
